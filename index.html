About Me

Born in 1997 in Oklahoma City. In 2015, I pursued Mass Communication in college before shifting to Real Estate for 4 years. Seeking for a more fulfilling career, I returned back to college with a goal to become a software engineer.

Who I Am

Currently, I am a student enrolled at the University of Central Oklahoma and pursuing a degree in software engineering with an anticipated graduation year of 2024. My journey in coding began when I taught myself the craft while working in the real estate industry. However, as I found more fulfillment in programming, I made the difficult decision to abandon my real estate career and sell my stake in a startup that I co-founded. The following semester, I returned to school to pursue my passion for coding.


Although my primary skillset is in C++, I have recently shifted my focus to learning other programming languages, including Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript. My long term goal is to work for or establish a company that aids local businesses in developing software solutions from the ground up.
